Pathein

Fascinating Facts

  • Pathein is famous for its colorful Pathein umbrellas, which are an important part of Myanmar's culture.
  • Ayar Stadium, which has 6,000 seats, is the main football venue in the city and the home ground of Ayeyarwady United FC, a team in the Myanmar National League.
  • Some Pathein umbrella workshops have been running for over 200 years and are still successful today.

Top three activities to do at Pathein

Attend a show or a concert

The Pathein Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a piknic in the local park

The Torshov Park is perfect for piknic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Play sports at the Lilleborg field

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights.
